ABOUT BVCOE MUN 2025

BVCOE MUN – Where Diplomacy Meets Excellence

BVCOE Model United Nations (MUN) is a premier platform that brings together passionate debaters, aspiring diplomats, and future leaders to engage in thought-provoking discussions on global issues. Hosted by Horizon – The Debating Society of Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, BVCOE MUN fosters intellectual discourse, critical thinking, and diplomatic skills among students.

With meticulously curated committees, engaging agendas, and experienced Executive Board members, BVCOE MUN promises an immersive experience where delegates can debate, negotiate, and craft impactful resolutions. Whether you are a seasoned MUN participant or a first-time delegate, BVCOE MUN offers the perfect stage to enhance your public speaking, negotiation, and problem-solving skills.

Join us in this journey of diplomacy and leadership, where your voice shapes the future. BVCOE MUN – Speak. Debate. Lead.

OUR COMMITTEES

Lok Sabha

Agenda: Deliberation upon "The Uniform Civil Code Bill, 2025" with emphasis on national cohesion and social stability.

About:

The Lok Sabha, or House of the People, is the lower house of India's bicameral Parliament. It serves as the primary legislative body representing citizens from constituencies across the nation, crafting laws that shape the future of the country.

United Nations General Assembly (UNGA)

Agenda: Ensuring sustainable management of water resources and access to safe drinking water and sanitation as a human right.

About:

The United Nations General Assembly is the main deliberative, policymaking, and representative organ of the UN. Comprising all 193 Member States, it provides a unique forum for multilateral discussion of international issues, playing a vital role in the process of standard-setting and the codification of international law.

International Press (IP)

Agenda: Covering all committee proceedings through journalistic reports, photography, and creative media.

About:

The International Press functions as the eyes and ears of the conference, documenting debates, interviews, and behind-the-scenes moments. Press delegates produce comprehensive coverage through articles, photographs, caricatures, and social media, offering unique perspectives on diplomatic proceedings.
